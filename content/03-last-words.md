<p class="lead">
This template is intentionally minimal — the goal is to get out of your way so you can focus on the content.
</p>

## What you have

<div class="kv-list">
  <div class="kv-row">
    <span class="kv-key">Content</span>
    <span class="kv-value">Plain <code>.md</code> files in <code>/content</code> — one per section</span>
  </div>
  <div class="kv-row">
    <span class="kv-key">Styling</span>
    <span class="kv-value">Tailwind CSS + custom prose rules in <code>globals.css</code></span>
  </div>
  <div class="kv-row">
    <span class="kv-key">Components</span>
    <span class="kv-value">Single <code>docs-layout.tsx</code> in <code>/components</code></span>
  </div>
  <div class="kv-row">
    <span class="kv-key">Output</span>
    <span class="kv-value">Static HTML in <code>/out</code> — deploy anywhere</span>
  </div>
</div>

## Where to go from here

<div class="steps">
  <div class="step">
    <div class="step-content">
      <strong>Replace the demo content</strong>
      <p>Delete everything in <code>/content</code> and add your own <code>.md</code> files. That's the whole job.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="step">
    <div class="step-content">
      <strong>Update the sidebar name</strong>
      <p>Open <code>/components/docs-layout.tsx</code> and change the author name and title at the top of the sidebar.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="step">
    <div class="step-content">
      <strong>Build and export</strong>
      <p>Run <code>npm run build</code>. Upload the <code>/out</code> folder to GitHub Pages, Netlify, Vercel, or any static host.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

## Hosting options

<div class="two-col">
  <div class="col">
    <h4>GitHub Pages <span class="badge badge-green">Free</span></h4>
    <p>Push <code>/out</code> to a <code>gh-pages</code> branch. Works out of the box with the static export.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="col">
    <h4>Netlify / Vercel <span class="badge badge-green">Free</span></h4>
    <p>Connect your repo and set the publish directory to <code>out</code>. Auto-deploys on every push.</p>
  </div>
</div>
